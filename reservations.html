Reservation Policy

Confirmation

All reservations will receive a confirmation email. Please check your spam folder if you do not receive it within 15 minutes of booking.

Arrival Time

We kindly ask that you arrive on time for your reservation. Tables will be held for 15 minutes past the reservation time, after which they may be released.

Cancellation

If you need to cancel or modify your reservation, please do so at least 24 hours in advance. Late cancellations or no-shows may incur a fee.

Large Parties

For groups of 9 or more, please contact us directly. A deposit may be required for large party reservations.

Special Events

For special events and holidays, a credit card is required to secure your reservation. Cancellation policies may vary for these occasions.

Children

Children are welcome at Soul Fine Dine. Please inform us if you will need high chairs or special accommodations for young guests.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a dress code?

We suggest smart casual attire. While we do not have a strict dress code, we kindly request that guests refrain from wearing athletic wear, beachwear, or flip-flops.

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Yes, we are happy to accommodate dietary restrictions and allergies. Please inform us of any special dietary needs when making your reservation or in the special requests section of the reservation form.

Is there a corkage fee?

We allow guests to bring their own wine with a corkage fee of ₹1,500 per bottle. Please note that we have an extensive wine list curated by our sommelier to complement our menu.

Do you offer valet parking?

Yes, complimentary valet parking is available for all our guests. Alternatively, there is public parking available within walking distance of the restaurant.

Can I make a reservation for the Chef's Table?

The Chef's Table is available by special reservation only and offers a unique multi-course tasting menu. Please contact us directly to inquire about availability and pricing.

Is there a time limit for dining?

We typically allocate 2 hours for parties of 2-4 and 2.5 hours for larger parties. If you anticipate needing more time, please let us know when making your reservation.
