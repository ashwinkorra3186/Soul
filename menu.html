At SOUL, our menu is a celebration of French culinary traditions reimagined through a contemporary lens. Each dish is meticulously crafted using the finest seasonal ingredients, with a focus on innovative techniques and harmonious flavor combinations.

Our Executive Chef Arjun Kapoor and his talented team draw inspiration from classical French gastronomy while incorporating modern approaches to create a dining experience that is both familiar and surprising.

Vegetarian options available upon request. Please inform your server of any dietary restrictions or allergies.
