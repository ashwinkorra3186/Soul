Our History

2018

The Beginning

SOUL was born from the vision of Chef Arjun Kapoor, who after years of training in Michelin-starred restaurants across France, returned with a dream to create a dining destination that would showcase French culinary traditions through a contemporary lens.

2019

Grand Opening

After months of meticulous planning and design, SOUL opened its doors in the heart of Jubilee Hills. The restaurant quickly gained recognition for its innovative approach to French cuisine and elegant ambiance.

2020

Adapting to Challenges

During the global pandemic, SOUL pivoted to create exclusive at-home dining experiences, bringing the restaurant's signature dishes to patrons' homes while maintaining the highest standards of quality and presentation.

2022

Culinary Recognition

SOUL was awarded "Best French Restaurant" and received accolades for its commitment to culinary excellence and innovative dining concepts.

2024

Expansion & Evolution

With a growing reputation for excellence, SOUL expanded its offerings to include a chef's table experience, seasonal tasting menus, and exclusive culinary events featuring guest chefs from France and around the world.

Present

Continuing the Journey

Today, SOUL continues to push the boundaries of culinary artistry, creating memorable dining experiences that celebrate the rich tapestry of French gastronomy while embracing contemporary sensibilities.

Our Culinary Team

Meet the passionate artisans behind our exceptional cuisine

Arjun Kapoor

Executive Chef & Founder

With over 15 years of experience in prestigious kitchens across France, Chef Arjun brings a wealth of knowledge and passion to SOUL. His innovative approach to French cuisine, combined with his dedication to perfection, forms the cornerstone of our culinary identity.

Sophia Rodriguez

Head Chef

A graduate of Le Cordon Bleu Paris, Chef Sophia specializes in avant-garde techniques that transform traditional French dishes into modern masterpieces. Her creative vision and technical precision are evident in every dish that leaves her kitchen.

Raj Sharma

Pastry Chef

Chef Raj's desserts are a perfect balance of artistry and flavor. Trained in Switzerland and France, he combines classical French pastry techniques with contemporary presentations to create desserts that are as visually stunning as they are delicious.

Aisha Patel

Head Sommelier

With a refined palate and extensive knowledge of French wines, Aisha curates our exceptional wine list and creates thoughtful pairings that enhance the dining experience. Her passion for wine education makes her an invaluable resource for guests seeking the perfect complement to their meal.
