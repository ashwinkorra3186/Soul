Frequently Asked Questions

Common inquiries about our private dining services

What is the minimum spend requirement for private dining?

Minimum spend requirements vary based on the dining space, day of the week, and time of year. The Cellar Room typically starts at ₹30,000, The Terrace at ₹50,000, and The Grand Room at ₹80,000. The Chef's Table Experience has a fixed price per person rather than a minimum spend. Please contact our events team for specific pricing based on your date and requirements.

How far in advance should I book my private dining event?

We recommend booking at least 2-4 weeks in advance for smaller gatherings and 1-3 months for larger events, especially during peak seasons (October-December and April-June). The Chef's Table Experience often books 1-2 months in advance. However, we always encourage you to inquire about availability even for last-minute requests, as we may be able to accommodate you.

Can I bring my own cake for a celebration?

Yes, you are welcome to bring your own cake for special celebrations. There is a cake service fee of ₹500 which includes cutting, plating, and serving. Alternatively, our pastry chef can create a custom cake for your event with advance notice, starting at ₹1,500.

Is there a deposit required to secure my booking?

Yes, a 50% deposit is required to confirm your private dining reservation. The remaining balance is due on the day of your event. For events with a value exceeding ₹1,00,000, we offer the option of a payment plan with a 30% initial deposit, 30% due 14 days before the event, and the remaining 40% due on the event day.

Can I arrange for special decorations or entertainment?

Absolutely! We welcome personal touches to make your event special. You may arrange for additional decorations, floral arrangements, or entertainment, subject to our venue guidelines. Our events team can also recommend trusted vendors or assist with arrangements for an additional coordination fee. Please discuss your ideas with us during the planning process.
