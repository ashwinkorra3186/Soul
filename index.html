Our Philosophy

My passion and vision for Soul is to bring authentic European culinary experiences to our guests. I sincerely desire to offer experiences that reflect Soulful curations and that you will discover an honest effort towards top-quality, healthy, and Soulful dining at Soul.

In accordance with customary and scientific dining practices, I recommend that guests consider the IV or V course meal or a curated degustation menu. The menu features timeless traditional European classics, contemporary curations from Soul (such as Lamb Ribs in Red Wine), and a few international chefs (such as Wellington from Chef Ramsay). Each serving is cooked fresh and live, in accordance with the concept of fresh and healthy dining. Nothing is pre- or half-done, except for ice cream and elements of desserts that require prior setting.

Each serving is cooked and plated personally, one at a time. The concept at Soul subscribes to slow dining, the European way. We request that our guests take time to enjoy delectable food, over great company and conversations, making treasures of memories at the table.
